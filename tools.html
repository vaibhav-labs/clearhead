Why these tools exist

Most career, money, and mental-load advice is generic because it has to be — the advisor doesn't know you. These tools split the difference: they take two minutes of honest input and hand back something specific. The free output is the diagnosis. The deeper unlock — the personalised audit, the framework, the pivot strategy — is one email or one conversation away.
