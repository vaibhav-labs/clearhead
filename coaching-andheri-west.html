Why some people choose in-person.

Video calls are fine for most things. But for a conversation about something you haven't said out loud before, a lot of people find it easier to be honest in a room than on a screen. No connection lag, no wondering if the other person's attention has drifted to a second tab. Just the two of you, in Lokhandwala, for an hour.

Neither format is treated as the "real" one here. Online sessions get exactly the same attention as in-person ones. This page exists because enough people specifically asked whether in-person was an option in Andheri West — the answer is yes, and here's what it actually looks like.
