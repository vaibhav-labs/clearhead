## Medium (4)
- **FAQ schema text doesn't match visible question** — `post-quiet-cracking.html:93` (schema) vs `:318` (visible) — schema asks "What actually helps with quiet cracking **and burnout**?" but the page shows "What actually helps with quiet cracking?". Google requires FAQ markup to match on-page text; align the two (drop "and burnout" from the schema, or add it to the visible `<summary>`).
- **FAQ schema text doesn't match visible question** — `post-cant-switch-off.html:91` (schema) vs `:311` (visible) — schema asks "Is not being able to switch off **from work** bad for your health?" but the page shows "Is not being able to switch off bad for your health?". Make the two strings identical.
- **Title too long (68 chars)** — `still-on-your-list.html:7` — "What Are You Still Putting Off? A Reflection Tool | Clearhead Mumbai" will be truncated in search results. Trim to ≤60 chars (e.g. "What Are You Still Putting Off? A Reflection Tool | Clearhead").
- **Meta description too long (183 chars)** — `still-on-your-list.html:8` — exceeds the ~160-char display limit and will be cut off. Tighten to 120–160 chars.

## Low (4)
- **Sitemap `lastmod` stale for tools.html** — `sitemap.xml:95` — shows `2026-05-30`, but `tools.html` was last changed `2026-06-14` (git). Update the `lastmod` to reflect the real edit date so crawlers re-fetch.
- **`still-on-your-list.html` missing OG image dimensions/alt** — `still-on-your-list.html:17` — it has 7 OG tags vs 10 on every other indexable page; it's missing `og:image:width`, `og:image:height`, and `og:image:alt`. Add them so social/link previews render reliably.
- **FAQ schema vs visible quote-style mismatch** — `post-lonely.html:69` (schema, `'unknown'`) vs `:233` (visible, `"unknown"`) — same words, different quote characters. Cosmetic, but for strict FAQ-match compliance make them identical.
- **LocalBusiness JSON-LD missing `priceRange`** — `index.html:80` (LocalBusiness block) — Google lists `priceRange` as a recommended LocalBusiness property; it's absent. Add e.g. `"priceRange": "₹₹"`.
