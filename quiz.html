This might not be the right fit right now.

Based on what you've shared, it sounds like you might benefit most from clinical or psychiatric care — and that's not something this practice offers. That's not a judgment; it's just an honest answer.

Coaching and counselling at Clearhead are for people who are fundamentally okay but want a confidential space to think. For clinical conditions or psychiatric needs, a licensed professional will be far better placed to help you.

A few places to start in India:

  • Your GP or a hospital psychiatry department
  • iCall (Tata Institute of Social Sciences) — 9152987821
  • Vandrevala Foundation (24/7) — 1860-2662-345
  • NIMHANS — nimhans.ac.in

If your situation changes and you feel this kind of space might be useful alongside your care, you're welcome to come back.
