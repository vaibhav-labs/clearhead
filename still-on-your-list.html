Before you start —

This isn’t a quiz with a score. It’s a few slow, honest questions about the things you’d quietly hoped for and somehow haven’t done.

You’ll pick up to three of them. Then for each, you’ll answer three short things — how long it’s been with you, what’s actually stopping you, and where you sit with it right now.

There are no wrong answers. The whole point is to say a few things out loud, even if just to yourself, for the first time in a while. You can stop at any point.
